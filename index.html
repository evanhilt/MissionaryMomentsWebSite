Missionary Moments

About the App

This app is inteded to give members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints a starting point for member-missionary work. It acts as a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) application but instead of having customers, user's have 'Friends' with whom they share the restored gospel at 'Events'. Events symbolize past missionary moments with a friend. The various categories of events should help foster ideas of how Latter-Day Saints can share the restored gospel with anyone they know.

Privacy Policy

Personal Information

No data is collected from the Application: "Missionary Moments". All user entered data is stored locally on the user's iPhone. There exists no external database. No user entered data passes over a network except perhaps in iCloud according to the user's iCloud preferences. Since no data is collected from the App, sharing information with 3rd parties is impossible.

Use of Information

Since no information is collected from this App there is not information to use.
